The Strategic Mandate

Why a Second, More Focused Workshop?

While the first US-Singapore workshop covers five broad themes related to the resilience of cyber-physical infrastructure systems, the second workshop in D.C. provides a focus inspired by two accelerating trends:

ACCELERATION 01

Disruptive Technologies

Rapid convergence of quantum engineering, decentralized autonomous robotics, and agentic AI. These offer breakthrough protective capabilities while simultaneously presenting unprecedented system vulnerability surfaces.

ACCELERATION 02

Compounding Hazards

Increasing frequency, severity, and cascading correlation of natural disasters (extreme climate events) and artificial, coordinated threat vectors (state-sponsored cyber assaults, complex physical sabotage).

Scientific Domains

Focus Areas & Technical Pillars

1

Quantum Engineering for Infrastructure Systems

Understanding what quantum means for infrastructure systems and exploring their opportunities and vulnerabilities.

2

Resilience Red Teaming

Constructing systematic adversarial frameworks and benchmarks to exploit hidden vulnerabilities and validating AI tools for safety-critical infrastructure applications.

3

Unconventional & Nature-Based Solutions

Blending nature-based solutions and exploiting hidden capacities and resources within infrastructure systems.
